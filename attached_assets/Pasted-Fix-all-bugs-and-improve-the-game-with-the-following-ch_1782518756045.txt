## BUG FIX 1 — PITCH FORMATION DIRECTION (CRITICAL)

The football pitch is upside down. Fix the player positions so:
- GK appears at the BOTTOM of the pitch (defending end)
- DEF line appears just above GK (second row from bottom)
- MID line appears in the middle
- FWD line appears at the TOP of the pitch (attacking end)

The pitch should read from bottom to top: GK → DEF → MID → FWD
This is the standard football formation view.

Also fix slot positions on the pitch for 4-3-3 or 4-2-3-1:
Bottom row: GK (center)
Second row: LB — CB — CB — RB
Third row: CDM — CM — CM (or LM — CM — RM)
Top row: LW — ST — RW
