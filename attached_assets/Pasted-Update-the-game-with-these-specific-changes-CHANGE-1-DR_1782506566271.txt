## CHANGE 1 — DRAFT / TEAM BUILDING SYSTEM (Full overhaul)

The current system is WRONG. Replace it completely with this:

### How drafting works:
1. Show the player a FULL squad of a RANDOMLY selected national team from any World Cup year (1930–2026)
   - Display: team name, year, and ALL players in the squad in a list
   - Each player shows: name, position (GK / DEF / MID / FWD)

2. The player CLICKS on any player from that list to add them to their team
   - The player's real position determines which slot it fills in the formation
   - Example: clicking Pelé (FWD) fills the next empty FWD slot
   - Example: clicking Beckenbauer (DEF) fills the next empty DEF slot

3. After selecting, a NEW random national team appears (different country/year)
   - The player picks ANOTHER player from that team

   - Example: if all 3 FWD slots are taken, FWD players in the next squad are GREYED OUT and unselectable
   - The player MUST pick from the available (unfilled) positions

4. Repeat until all 11 slots are filled:
   - GK × 1
   - DEF × 4 (CB × 2, LB × 1, RB × 1)
   - MID × 4 (CDM × 1, CM × 2, CAM × 1)  
   - FWD × 2 (LW × 1 or RW × 1, ST × 1)
   (or use 4-3-3: GK, 4 DEF, 3 MID, 3 FWD)

5. Rules:
   - Cannot change a player after selecting
   - Greyed-out players = position already full — cannot be selected
   - The same team can appear again (it's random each time)
   - Show the current team formation on the side updating in real time as picks are made

### UI for draft screen:
- LEFT side: current formation (football pitch view, updating live)
- RIGHT side: current random squad list (scrollable, greyed items for filled positions)
- Show which positions still need filling at the top
