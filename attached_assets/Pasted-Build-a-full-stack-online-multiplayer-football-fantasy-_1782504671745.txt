Build a full-stack online multiplayer football fantasy game called "World Cup Fantasy Manager" using React + Node.js (or Next.js). Here are the complete specifications:

---

## GAME CONCEPT
Two players compete by each building a team of 11 players chosen from real World Cup squads (1930–2026), then simulating matches against AI teams and each other.

---

## MULTIPLAYER & LOBBY SYSTEM
- A player can either:
  - **Search for opponent**: matchmaking queue
  - **Create private room**: generates a 6-character share code (e.g. "WC4829")
  - **Join with code**: enter friend's code to join their room
- Both players see each other's usernames in the lobby
- Game starts only when both players are ready

---

## TEAM BUILDING PHASE (Draft Mode)

### How it works:
1. Player is shown a **randomly selected national team** from any World Cup edition (1930–2026)
2. Player must pick **one player** from that squad for a **specific position** on the pitch
3. After picking, a **new random national team** from a different tournament year appears
4. Player continues until all 11 positions are filled
5. Player **cannot change a player after selecting them**
6. Player **cannot place a player in the wrong position** (must match the player's real position)

### Positions to fill (4-3-3 or 4-2-3-1 formation):
- GK × 1
- CB × 2
- LB × 1, RB × 1
- CDM × 1 (or CM)
- CM × 2
- LW × 1, RW × 1
- ST × 1

### Position rules:
- GK → only GKs can be placed here
- CB/LB/RB → only defenders
- CDM/CM → only midfielders (CDM, CM, CAM count as midfielders)
- LW/RW/ST → only forwards/attackers
- The game must enforce this — no exceptions

### Player rating system:
Each player gets a base rating based on:
- Tournament performance (goals, assists, clean sheets for GK/DEF)
- Historical reputation (Pelé, Maradona, Ronaldo, Messi etc. get boosted ratings)
- Position role weight
- Era adjustment (earlier eras get slight adjustment for era quality)
- Rating range: 60–99

---

## TOURNAMENT SIMULATION

### Group Stage Draw:
After both players finish building their teams, the game auto-generates a group stage:
- 4 groups of 4 teams each
- Each group contains:
  - Player's custom team
  - 3 randomly drawn **real historical World Cup national teams** (e.g., "Germany 2014", "Brazil 1970")
- VS player's group also has their custom team + 3 random historical teams
- Players may end up in the same group or different groups

### Match Simulation:
- Each match simulates 90 minutes compressed into **30 seconds of real time**
- Show a live mini-scoreboard that updates with goals as they "happen" during those 30 seconds
- Match result is calculated based on:
  - Overall team rating (average of all 11 players)
  - Tactical bonuses (e.g., balanced formation, era diversity bonus)
  - Random factor (15–25% variance to simulate upsets)
  - Home/Away factor (slight boost to higher-rated team)

### Match Display:
- Show minute-by-minute event tickers (goal scored at 23', yellow card at 45', etc.)
- Final score + scorers shown
- Points: Win = 3pts, Draw = 1pt, Loss = 0pt

### Knockout Stage:
- Top 2 from each group advance
- Single-elimination: Round of 8 → Semi-finals → Final
- VS mode: if both players qualify, they are placed in opposite brackets and can meet in the final

### Winner:
- The player whose custom team wins the tournament is the overall winner
- If vs mode, whoever reaches the furthest stage wins the head-to-head
